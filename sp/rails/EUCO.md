Nearly identical to its New World counterpart. Stands by the edge of the water a lot.

<hr class="thin-line">

**Appearance:** black-and-white rail that resembles a semi-aquatic chicken. Beak is white, as is the shield structure above. **Lacks a red callus**. Eyes are red. Feet are lobed with flanges along each toe.

**Voice:** haven't heard anything from these guys yet.

**Behaviour:** swims and presumbably eats aquatic vegetation. Rests on the shore when not swimming.

**Known whereabouts:** near water such as ponds.

**Seen in:** Harbin
