**Appearance:** small-ish tern. Head has a typical black cap, and the face and vent are white. Middle torso and long wings are distinctively dark grey. The beak is sharp and pointed, red in colour.

**Voice:** haven't heard anything from these guys yet.

**Behaviour:** flies quickly over water before diving to catch small fish. 

**Known whereabouts:** rivers

**Seen in:** Harbin
