Distinct waterbird that may be confused with a duck. But it is not a duck.

<hr class="thin-line">

**Appearance:** appears duck-like from a distance. Large grebe; black above and light grey below, with a red neck, grey cheeks, and black cap in breeding plumage. Nonbreeding and immature appearance lacks red. Bill is long-sharp, and has some yellow.

**Voice:** duet sounds like "ekekekekeke—ÆUUUUGH". Can emit various clucking sounds as well.

**Behaviour:** excellent diver, will swim at the surface before plunging its head into the water and diving for prey or to evade threats. Stays underwater for several seconds before popping back up.

**Known whereabouts:** wetlands, lakes

**First seen in:** Edmonton, Wabanum
