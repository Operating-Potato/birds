The meep meep upside down bird (i love them very much because they are very wonderful)

:)

**Appearance:** small scansorial songbird, slate-grey above and reddish-orange on the bottom. Face is pale, with thick black streaks covering the eyes.

**Voice:** "meep meep meep," "meeerp, meeerp"

**Behaviour:** climbs trees and runs down trunks headfirst, as well as hanging upside-down from a branch. Pries seeds and insects from crevices. Can be seen in a L-shaped position, with the body facing towards the ground while the head is tilted upwards.

**Known whereabouts:** near coniferous trees.

**Seen in:** Edmonton, Wabanum
