A very melodious thrush. It looks vaguely like a robin, though I'd say all thrushes look like robins to some degree.

<hr class="thin-line">

__Appearance:__ as the name suggests, the Grey-backed Thrush has a grey back, as well as a grey head and rump. The underparts are white, with orange flanks. Female and immature individuals have a browner, more streaky colouration. The bill is a bright yellow. Medium sized.

__Voice:__ song is a deep-ish "churp-churp-churp chpito" or "chipito chipito churp chkchkchk"

__Behaviour:__ can be seen singing loudly from a perch, or hopping on the ground. Relatively approachable.

__Known wherabouts:__ forests and parks.

__Seen in:__ Harbin
